Our Background

Sewosy Malaysia, since its inception in 2007, has been one of the leading manufacturers of security related products in Malaysia.

In affiliation with Sewosy France, a recognized name in the manufacturing and distributing of access control products in Europe,

Sewosy Malaysia offers a comprehensive line of products such as electromagnetic locks, magnetic lock door handles, magnetic door holders and other door access products and accessories.

Philosophy and Business Commitment

Sewosy has proven over the years to be a total reliable partner with true commitment, dedication and integrity to forge a long term successful business relationship with its partners.

Sewosy will focus on building and strengthening confidence, trust and innovation of its products for ultimate customers' satisfaction and loyalty.
